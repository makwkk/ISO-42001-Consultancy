Our process

The 90-day path to certification

A predictable, fixed-scope delivery model used across our 40+ engagements.

  1. 01

    Scoping

    We define the AI systems in scope, your organisational context, and the regulatory landscape.

  2. 02

    Gap Analysis

    Current-state assessment against every ISO 42001 clause. Output: remediation roadmap.

  3. 03

    Design & Build

    Policy, SoA, risk register, controls, SOPs. All artefacts are yours to keep.

  4. 04

    Operate & Train

    Teams run the AIMS for 30 days. We deliver training and management review.

  5. 05

    Internal Audit

    Independent audit. We close all findings before the certification body arrives.

  6. 06

    Certification

    Stage 1 + Stage 2 audit support. 100% first-pass success rate to date.
